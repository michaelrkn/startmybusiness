Choose a company structure

The first thing you'll need to do is choose a company structure. For the vast majority of new companies, the best choice is a corporation taking an S election (called an "S corporation") or an LLC. LLCs have the advantage of slightly less paperwork, but it can be difficult to add or remove owners and can be harder to raise investment. S corporations have slightly more paperwork, but can easily change owners and aren't limited in raising investment.

If you're not sure, we recommend you start an S corporation, because the additional paperwork is minimal, and it doesn't constrain your ownership changes or growth.
